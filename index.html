About Me

I am Luyanda Gamaye, a problem-solver dedicated to humanizing technology. My journey began in IT Support, where I developed deep empathy for user frustrations and a systematic approach to solving them. This foundation led me to Software Development, where I gained proficiency in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to build practical solutions. My goal is to ensure technology serves the human using it, crafting products that are a genuine delight to interact with.
